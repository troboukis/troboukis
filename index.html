Data journalism projects made during the Lede Program of Columbia Journalism School, in the summer of 2018.

Animal Research Project

In 2016 there was a 7% increase in use of animals in research labs across the United States. These are the most commonly used animals in research labs.

Mental Health Crisis in NYC Jails

More than 7 out of 10 women and 4 out of 10 men are put under mental observation in NYC jails. A look into the population of NYC correctional system.

Spills in New York

Petroleum and other hazardous material end up in Hudson River and other waterfronts in the State of New York. A look into the data reveal what kind of spills are most common in the State.
